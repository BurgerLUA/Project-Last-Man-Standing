Foreword

This book is meant to be a simple list of things to do just in case you're new or forgetful or something. Follow the list, and you'll be ready for deployment in no time!

Step 1: Wake Up!

Depending on how your body got here, you might be dead, new, or stored away from a previous mission. If you're new and immediately panicking because I brought up death, don't worry! NanoTrasen keeps the latest copy of your DNA on a super-secure megaserver so they can clone you at any time! If you die, the chip in your brain that they implanted at birth will instantaneously send your current memories and your DNA structure to the nearest satellite for future use! In rare instances of sudden planet destruction, NanoTrasen will use the latest backup!
