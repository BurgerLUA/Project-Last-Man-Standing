Increasing your Ranged Weapons skill improves your accuracy and reduces your miss chance with all ranged weapons.
Shooting a Ranged Weapon while moving decreases your accuracy. Remain still to get the best shot!
Shooting a Ranged Weapon in quick succession reduces your accuracy with every shot. To maintain accuracy, shoot in short bursts.
Headshots deal triple damage.
Surplus ammo is cheaper and easier to find, however it deals much less damage than it's regular counterpart.
Hollow point rounds deal very high damage against unarmored targets, however has little to no armor penetration.
Armor piercing rounds deal low damage, however it pierces through armor like butter.
Casings can be reused to remake ammo at a portable ammo crafting bench.
